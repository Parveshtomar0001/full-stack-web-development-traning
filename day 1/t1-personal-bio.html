The lion, often referred to as the "king of the jungle," is one of the most powerful and majestic animals in the animal kingdom. It is a large carnivorous mammal belonging to the cat family, scientifically known as Panthera leo. Lions are known for their muscular build, deep roars, and golden-brown coats. Male lions are easily recognized by their thick manes, which vary in color and size. They typically live in groups called prides, which consist of related females, their offspring, and a few adult males. Lions are native to parts of Africa and a small population exists in the Gir Forest of India. As apex predators, they play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystem.
